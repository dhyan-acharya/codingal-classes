Lions (Panthera leo) are large, carnivorous mammals and one of the world's most iconic apex predators. Famed as the "king of the beasts," they are highly social felines that live in family groups called prides, and are primarily found in the grasslands, savannas, and shrublands of Sub-Saharan Africa and a small region in India.
